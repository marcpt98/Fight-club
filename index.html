Fight Club: Art Of Fighting tribute

What is Art Of Fighting?

Art Of Fighting is a martial art game created by Capcom in 1987. In it, we are placed in Southtown, a city in which Ryo and Robert, two karate students, have to save Ryo's sister, Yuri, who has been kidnapped by a misterious fighter.

How to play (controls):

Keyboard (Player 1):

A: Move left.
D: Move right.
W: Jump.
S: Crouch.
T: Punch.
R: Kick.
S+T: Crouch Punch.
S+R: Crouch Kick.
F: Special attack (Hadouken).
F4: Insta Kill.

Keyboard (Player 2):

J: Move left.
L: Move right.
I: Jump.
K: Crouch.
O: Punch.
P: Kick.
K+O: Crouch Punch.
K+P: Crouch Kick.
U: Special attack (Hadouken).
F8: Insta kill.

Other interesting keyboard controls:

F3: Show collisions on screen with colours.
F5: God mode (players without collisions).
SPACE: Change scene.
ESC: Exit game.

Gamepad:

Joystick right: Move forward.
Joystick left: Move backward.
Joystick up: Jump.
Joystick down: Crouch.
X: Punch
Y: Kick
B: Low kick
Joystick down + X: Punch crouch.
Joystick down + Y: Kick crouch.
Joystick up + X: Jump punch.
Joystick up + Y: Jump Kick.
START: Pass scene.
BACK: Exit game.
